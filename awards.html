Honors & Awards

3rd Place Winner–Creative Excellence Initiative

Jordan Design and Development Bureau (JODDB)

Mamba Team Achieves Third Place in Jordan Design and Development Bureau's Creative Excellence Initiative! Following this achievement, we collaborated with JODDB to develop a fully functional mine detection robot prototype. Our work included integrating advanced features utilizing robotics and artificial intelligence, enhancing its capabilities for defensive military missions, and contributing to human life preservation.

Appreciation for Efforts Minesweepers Competition, Egypt

Minesweepers Competition, Egypt

Received an Appreciation Award at the Minesweepers Competition in Egypt, representing Jordan as part of the Mamba Team. Recognized for developing an innovative mine detection robot.To see our video click here.

3rd Place–Best Mechanical Design

Firefighter Competition-PUST

After months of dedication, challenges, and breakthroughs, our robot earned 3rd Place in the Mechanical Design category at the Firefighting Competition hosted by Princess Sumaya University for Technology (PSUT), recognizing its innovative and reliable engineering design.

Appreciation for Participation

Sumo Robot Competition-HTU

Received an Appreciation Award for participating three consecutive times in the Sumo Robot Competition at HTU. This ongoing journey strengthened our creativity, teamwork, and engineering problem-solving skills.

Participation in NASA Space Apps Challange

NASA Space Apps-HTU

Participated in the NASA Space Apps Challenge at HTU, an inspiring experience that fostered creativity, teamwork, and innovation. Collaborated with a diverse team to tackle real-world challenges and explore solutions inspired by space science and technology.

Participation in motionSense AI competition

MathWorks

Participated in the MotionSense AI Competition by MathWorks, where we developed a Flutter application to stream sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer) in real-time to a server. An AI model then analyzed the data to classify different movement patterns such as walking, running, and standing.
